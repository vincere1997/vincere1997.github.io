Welcome to my personal homepage!

I am a Ph.D. student (2023-) at [the School of Computer Science and Engineering](https://cs.njust.edu.cn/), [Nanjing University of Science and Technology](https://www.njust.edu.cn/).
I am a member of [the Knowledge Mining Group (KMG)](http://www.njustkmg.cn/), supervised by Prof. [Yang Yang](https://gsmis.njust.edu.cn/open/TutorInfo.aspx?dsbh=KRCktKdhugFyptSFE9ocvQ==&yxsh=z70ppxVSQAs=&zydm=SwsWR9zpmmw=).
Previously, I received my M.Eng. degree (2020–2023) from [Nanjing Forestry University](https://www.njfu.edu.cn/), advised by Assoc. Prof. [Demin Gao](https://it.njfu.edu.cn/szll/fjs/20240311/i300388.html).

My research interests include data mining and deep learning, with a current focus on multimodal learning and its applications.
